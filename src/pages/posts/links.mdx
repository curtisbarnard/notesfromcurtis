## Japan
- [Japanese zoning](https://urbankchoze.blogspot.com/2014/04/japanese-zoning.html): This would be good to read along with the book [Emergent Tokyo](https://oroeditions.com/product/emergent-tokyo)
- [Japanese Culinary Academy eBooks](https://www.kpu.ac.jp/jp_cuisine_ebook/): If you really wanted to nerd out on Japanese cooking then have a look at these books hosted on Kyoto Prefectural University's website.

## Japanese
- [Jiten](https://jiten.moe/): For helping me find new japanese content that is around my level. It allows importing of your anki stats so you can see the percentage of characters known for a given work.
- [NHK Kokokoza](https://edu.web.nhk/kokokoza/): NHK high school “courses” that would be good for additional Japanese studying.
- [Japanese Graded Readers](https://tadoku.org/japanese/en/free-books-en/): Free Japanese graded readers. One of my favorite resources for learning Japanese. They also have a list of non-free books, which I've been able to check out from my local library. 
- [Gaku: Minna no Yama](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gaku:_Minna_no_Yama): A manga about mountaineering, I haven't read it yet.
